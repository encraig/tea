Credit cards must be issued in the U.S.A.

Credit Card Authorization

Orders will be processed and shipped pending credit card authorization. When an order is placed, Tea will authorize your credit card to verify the funds are available. If you are using MasterCard or Visa, the authorization may appear as if you have been charged. With some card companies, the funds become unavailable after authorization. However, your account will not be charged until the order actually ships. If your order is canceled, the hold on the funds will be released and the funds will become available in less than a week or depending on the issuer of the card.

Tea Collection is happy to provide a one-time price adjustment on items you’ve purchased if you see the item on our site offered for a lower price within 10 days of placing your order. Gift Wrap

Gift wrapping is included in the checkout options. We'll wrap your special gift in our furoshiki, inspired by the artful Japanese tradition of wrapping gifts in large, beautiful swaths of cloth. Your gift will be wrapped in a classic Japanese printed cloth, and we'll enclose a note card with your personal message.

We also offer the option to purchase the gift wrap supplies. This provides flexibility if you want to buy gifts for multiple children on one order. Gift wrap service adds $8 to your order and gift wrap supplies adds $5.

Learn how to use and reuse our Furoshiki Wrap.

If your package is a gift but you do not wish to purchase gift packaging, you can send a complimentary gift message that will print on the packing list that is included with each order.

Packing lists serve as gift receipts. (They don't include prices.)

Important Kentucky Sales And Use Tax Information

Tea Collection is not required to and does not collect Kentucky sales or use tax. Your purchase may be subject to Kentucky use tax unless the purchase is exempt from taxation in Kentucky. However, your purchase is not exempt merely because it is made over the internet.

The Commonwealth of Kentucky requires Kentucky purchasers to report all purchases of tangible personal property or digital property that are not taxed by the retailer and pay use tax on those purchases unless exempt under Kentucky law. The tax may be reported and paid on the Kentucky individual income tax return or by filing a consumer use tax return with the Kentucky Department of Revenue.

For more information, please visit http://revenue.ky.gov/individual/usetax.htm
